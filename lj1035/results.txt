From 2007 to 2012, there is no significant change in the income distribution among all regions in the world. For Africa, both the mean and spread of income remain at a stable level. For Asia, the income increase steadily from 2007 to 2010, experiences a downturn in 2011 and finally recovers in 2012. For Europe, there is no significant pattern change in its income distribution but we can observe a slightly decrease in its average. For North America, the overall income level follows a decreasing trend. For Oceania, the income distribution remains stable from 2007 to 2011 but there is a sharp decrease in its maximum income level from 2011 to 2012. For South America, the overall income level follows an increasing trend and the changing pattern is relatively smooth.
