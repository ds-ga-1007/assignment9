This can be used as an indicator of poverty. in 2011 the number of countries with per capita GDP less than 3000 dropped significantly in both Africa and Asia. However, in 2012 the number of countries in Africa rose again while Asia continued to decrease. Also in 2012, the number of countries with GDP per capita less than 3000 decreased in North America.\
