Major changes over these 6 years:

1. From 2007 to 2008: 
In Europian countries income per person reduces both on average and on median, as shown by the histograms and the box plots. 
The inter-quartile range of the Oceania countries increased.

2. From 2008 to 2009: 
Europe and north america have lower medians. Also averge income decreased for Europe and North America.

3. From 2009 to 2010: 
Reduction of income countinues in North America and Europe 
Asia increased on average in income per person.

4. From 2011 to 2012: 
Oceania and Europe have lower medians and 3rd quartiles. 

Overall, it seems that Europe and North America has been more affected by the financial crisis in 2008. However, Europe and Oceania seem to have a harder time recovering from the crisis compared to North America and other continents. Asia seems to be recovered from the crisis on average.
